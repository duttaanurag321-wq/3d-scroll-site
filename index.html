We are a RBI-registered financial services intermediary, founded with a singular purpose — to make credit access simple, honest, and efficient for every Indian borrower.

Our Story

Why we exist

India's credit gap is not a supply problem — it's a mismatch problem. Lenders have capital. Borrowers have need. But the system in between is opaque, fragmented, and often biased against the uninformed.

FINS Capital was founded in 2018 by a team of bankers and technologists who saw this clearly. Our mission was to build an intermediary that is genuinely neutral — one that earns its fee only when the borrower gets a good deal.

Today, we work with 40+ lenders across India, have served over 1.8 lakh customers, and maintain a 97% sanction rate for applications we formally submit — because we only submit when we're confident.

Our Principles

What we stand for

Three values that govern every decision we make — from the advice we give to the lenders we partner with.

Radical Transparency

We disclose our compensation model upfront. We tell you exactly which lenders we work with and why. We never push a product that isn't right for you — even if it pays us more.

Lender Agnosticism

Our recommendation algorithm is designed to find your best offer — not our most profitable one. We are empanelled with 40+ lenders specifically so we are never dependent on any single relationship.

Long-Term Thinking

A customer who gets a bad loan does not return. A customer who gets a great deal — and knows it — becomes a referral source for life. Our business model is built on lifetime value, not short-term commissions.
